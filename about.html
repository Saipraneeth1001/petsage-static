About PetSage

PetSage was born from a deeply personal experience.

Two years ago, my dog gradually lost her vision due to tick fever and inherited glaucoma. During that time, I visited multiple veterinary hospitals, consulted many doctors, and spent a significant amount of money — often just to ask small follow-up questions.

Despite all of this, I often felt uncertain, confused, and alone. I wished there was a way to get structured guidance, understand symptoms better, and recognize warning signs early — before rushing into expensive consultations.

PetSage is built to address that gap.

It helps pet parents:

  • Describe symptoms clearly
  • Upload photos for visual context
  • Track health history over time
  • Understand when a situation may need urgent veterinary attention

PetSage is not a doctor. It does not diagnose, treat, or prescribe. Instead, it acts as a thoughtful filter — helping pet parents make informed decisions and approach veterinarians better prepared.

Our goal is simple: reduce uncertainty, improve awareness, and help pets get timely care.

— Team PetSage

Delete your PetSage account

For security, we don’t delete an account just because someone typed an email address. You must either confirm via a secure link, or contact support from the same email address.

Users can request account deletion by emailing admin@petsage.in.
We delete all account data within 7 days.
Stored data includes email, chat history, pet profiles.
Some logs may be retained for legal/security purposes.

How to request deletion

  1. Send an email to admin@petsage.in from the email address you used to sign in.
  2. In the email body, include the email address you want us to delete (if different), and the words “Delete my account”.
